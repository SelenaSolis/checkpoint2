The Alley Cat


The Alley Cat consists of an asymmetrical, choppy, punk rock fur trim. Animal-safe fur coloring is an option for the more daring pets. Hunting in the alley can have a less than desireable affect on the claws, so a mani/pedi is a must.
"My cat used to just look like a normal indoor cat. Now she looks like she belongs in a cat rock band. In fact, she was asked to join a Misfits cover band, 'The Hissfits'. We could not be happier." -Momo Austin, TX
